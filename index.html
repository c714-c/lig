About, and Why Us?

At LIG, we are devoted to the distribution, restoration and the ability to access games. While physical media is currently only available for purchase in-store, we offer lots of hardware and software that come from many countries, and many brands.


Our team is made up of passionate gamers and tech enthusiasts who are dedicated to providing the best gaming experience possible. Founded in 2000, LIG has grown from a small local store selling card games to a leading competitor to major gaming retailers. Our team consists of 20 in-store employees and 32 employees working remotely, or with logistics. We only serve United States customers, but in the future we plan to expand to other countries. We are committed to providing excellent customer service and support, and we strive to create a welcoming and inclusive community for gamers of all backgrounds and skill levels.
